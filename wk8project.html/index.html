Why Keep Poultry?

Poultry farming is a profitable and sustainable business that provides a steady income while ensuring a reliable source of nutrition. Whether for eggs, meat, or breeding, poultry farming can be started with minimal capital and scaled up over time.

Benefits of Poultry Farming:

  • High demand for eggs and chicken meat
  • Low startup costs and fast returns
  • Easy to maintain and manage
  • Contributes to food security
